I am an Assistant Professor in the [Department of Statistics](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/sci/statistics/), University of Warwick. From 2021 to 2023, I was a postdoctoral researcher, hosted by [Tengyao Wang](https://personal.lse.ac.uk/wangt60/), and then an LSE Fellow from 2023 to 2024 at the London School of Economics and Political Science.

I completed my PhD in Statistics at the University of Cambridge, supervised by [Richard J. Samworth](http://www.statslab.cam.ac.uk/~rjs57/) and [Tengyao Wang](https://personal.lse.ac.uk/wangt60/), with the thesis title *High-dimensional Online Changepoint Detection*. My research interests include changepoint detection, high-dimensional statistics, robust statistics, online algorithms, machine learning, spatial econometrics and large language models.

## Interests

- High-dimensional statistics
- Changepoint detection
- Robust statistics
- Online algorithms
- Machine learning
- Spatial econometrics
- Large language models
