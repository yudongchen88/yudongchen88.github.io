About Me

I am a research officer in the Department of Statistics, London School of Economics and Political Science, hosted by Tengyao Wang. I completed my PhD in Statistics in University of Cambridge, supervised by Richard J. Samworth and Tengyao Wang, with the thesis title High-dimensional Online Changepoint Detection. My research interests include online and offline changepoint problems, high-dimensional statistics, robust statistics and machine learning.
